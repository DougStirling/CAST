The two draft e-books below have been created to help teachers and students of the New Zealand National Certificate of Educational Achievement.

The material is at an early stage of development and will change after feedback is obtained from teachers.

The following e-books have been customised for students and teachers of the NCEA subject Statistics and Modelling in New Zealand.

E-book for NCEA students

CAST for NCEA students
This e-book contains chapters that should help you to study for some NCEA modules.

Click the tab Public e-books to see the full collection CAST e-books. The public introductory e-books include more advanced material than is necessary for NCEA.

Resources for teachers of NCEA

CAST resources for teachers
This e-book is a collection of dynamic and interactive diagrams that can be used with a data projector to help teach some of the NCEA modules in class. The diagrams are structured into chapters corresponding to NCEA modules.
