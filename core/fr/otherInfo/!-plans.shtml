Future plans for CAST

Main introductory CAST e-books

There are now video versions of all pages in the three introductory statistics e-books. I intend to revise these video pages before the next release of CAST.

Exercises

CAST contains an e-book of exercises but more are needed for the later topics in the introductory e-books.

Advanced e-books

Release 5.5 of CAST contains advanced e-books on:

  • Multiple regression and general linear models
  • Design and analysis of experiments in agriculture and biology
  • Design and analysis of industrial experiments
  • Mathematical statistics

Summary versions of the pages in the e-books about regression and experiments would be useful.

At some point in the future, it is also hoped to write an e-book about multivariate methods — the CAST approach has considerable potential for explaining them in a non-mathematical way.

It is also hoped to write an e-book about official statistics. A single chapter has been written at this stage about data presentation in tables, graphs and maps — it is included as an 'e-booklet' rather than an e-book. It is hoped to extend this e-book with chapters about topics such as index numbers, demography, time series, surveys and national accounts.

These developments are dependent on funding.

Customisation and translation

The introductory CAST e-books have now been restructured to make translation into other languages easier. Projects have started to translate introductory CAST e-books into German and Spanish, with a French translation also in the pipeline. Volunteers to help with these or other translations are welcome.

Creation of customised e-books is now also possible. Hopefully others will contribute to the project by uploading customised e-books to be shared more widely.
