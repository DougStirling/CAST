This CAST e-book is closely related to the Business e-book for self-study by students. It contains the interactive diagrams (applets) from that e-book, but the surrounding explanatory text has been removed.

  • The lecturer should discuss the diagrams during the class.
  • There is a button under each applet that can be used to provide notes about how the applet might be used in a lecture.
  • Refer to the Business e-book for students for more background and explanation
