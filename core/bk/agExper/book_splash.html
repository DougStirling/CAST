This e-learning book describes principles and methods for the design and analysis of experiments.

Particular emphasis is placed on experiments in agricultural and biology though the methods covered here are also used in many other fields.

This e-book was partially funded by the Regional Universities Forum (RUFORUM) in East Africa and was written with substantial input from Roger Stern at the Statistical Services Centre at the University of Reading, England.

A companion e-book describes some further topics that are important in Industrial Experiments.
