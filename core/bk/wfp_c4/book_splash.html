Welcome to CAST for WFP, a version of CAST that is designed for staff in the World Food Programme.

This is the fourth e-book in the CAST for WFP series and contains advanced material from other versions of CAST that does not appear in the first three WFP e-books.

This e-book is a collection of sections that has not yet been structured into a coherent course of study but may be of interest to some readers.

As with the other e-books in the series, this e-book contains material from the CAST web site.
