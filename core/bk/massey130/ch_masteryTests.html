The initial chapters of this CAST e-book overlap with the New Zealand high school syllabus (NCEA).

In the Massey University paper 161.120, this initial material is assessed by three online mastery tests:

Mastery Test 1
This test examines the material in Chapters 2 and 3 of the e-book — basic graphical and numerical summaries of a single variable.
Mastery Test 2
The second test examines the material in Chapters 4 and 5 — relationships between two numerical variables, contingency tables and time series.
Mastery Test 3
The last test examines the material in Chapters 6, 7 and 8 — probability, normal distributions and binomial distributions.

The three sections below contain questions that are almost identical to those that will be asked in the mastery tests, but they give more help and feedback about wrong answers.

We strongly encourage you to try the exercises in this chapter before attempting the mastery tests.
