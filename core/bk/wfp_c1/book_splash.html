Welcome to CAST for WFP. This is the first of a series of e-learning books that have been created to help staff at the World Food Programme understand and use basic statistical methods.

This book describes the structure of data sets and simple ways to summarise and describe a categorical variable.

Follow-on e-learning books describe numerical and graphical ways to explore data, and basic inference.

This version of CAST was prepared in collaboration with Roger Stern from the Statistical Services Centre at the University of Reading, England. Customisation was funded by the World Food Programme.
