General linear model

The general linear model is

y  =  β0  + β1 x1  + β2 x2  + β3 x3  +  ...  +  ε

where

ε   ~   normal (0, σ)

Parameter estimates and standard errors

The best estimates of β0, β1, ... are the least squares estimates, b0, b1, ...
