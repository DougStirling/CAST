This e-book teaches the statistical methods and concepts in the Massey University paper 161.101 Statistics for Business.

The e-book contains more detail than you will need to know for the assignments, test and exam.

Some pages are intended to help you understand concepts even though this understanding will not be directly examined.

We hope that this extra material will help you to understand why statistical methods are used, and not simply give a "cookbook of recipes".

The final page in each chapter describes what we expect you to be able to do in the assignments, test and exam after studying that section of the course.
