This chapter explains some statistical concepts related to the NCEA level 3 standard "3.11: Conduct an experiment to investigate a situation using experimental design principles". It is usually taught at Year 13 in New Zealand high schools and is assessed at NCEA Level 3.

This chapter describes some principles that should be followed when designing an experiment.

The interactive diagrams are intended for teachers to use on data projectors.

Assumed background

The idea of experiments was introduced at NCEA level 2 in the chapter Conduct an Experiment. It explains why a well-designed experiment is needed before in order to understand how changing one variable affects another. Make sure that your students fully understand this material before reading further.

The material does not cover the complete curriculum. It has been provided as support material for parts of the curriculum.
