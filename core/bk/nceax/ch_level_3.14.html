This chapter explains some statistical concepts related to the NCEA level 3 standard "3.14: Apply probability distributions in solving problems". It is usually taught at Year 13 in New Zealand high schools and is assessed at NCEA Level 3.

This chapter describes the binomial distribution and gives some properties of sums and differences of random quantities.

The interactive diagrams are intended for teachers to use on data projectors.

Assumed background

This chapter assumes a sound understanding of probability and normal distributions. Revise these in the chapter Use Probability before starting to study the material in this chapter.

The material does not cover the complete curriculum. It has been provided as support material for parts of the curriculum.
