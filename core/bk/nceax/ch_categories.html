This chapter describes simple methods to summarise categorical data with frequency tables, pie charts and bar charts.

This material is mostly taught before Year 11 in New Zealand high schools so teachers may find it helpful when teaching statistics in Years 9 and 10 (or even earlier). In Year 11, it should be mainly revision of material that has already been taught but some pages are likely to provide extra information about specific methods. (There is some variability in the coverage of topics in Years 9 and 10 between schools.)

The material in this chapter can be used to support the teaching of statistical methods up to Year 10.

It can also be used for revision within NCEA Level 1.

The chapter contains interactive diagrams for teachers to use on data projectors.

The material does not cover the complete curriculum. It has been provided as support material for parts of the curriculum.
