This chapter explains some statistical concepts related to the NCEA level 2 standard "2.10: Conduct an experiment to investigate a situation using statistical methods".

This chapter explains the distinction between observational studies and experiments and explains why causal relationships can only be inferred from experiments.

The interactive diagrams are intended for teachers to use on data projectors.

Assumed background

Scatterplots and contingency tables are used to describe some concepts in this chapter

  • Understanding relationships
