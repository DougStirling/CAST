Welcome to CAST for NCEA. This 'e-book' contains a set of resources for teachers of the New Zealand National Certificate of Educational Achievement (NCEA).

Each page contains a dynamic diagram that can be used to help explain some concept in the curriculum. The diagrams should also make lessons more interesting. They are formatted for use with a data projector and are therefore fairly large with little supporting text visible. However there are buttons that expand to give advice on the most effective way to use the diagrams.

Coverage of the curriculum

This e-book does not cover everything in the curriculum or that must be learned for the NCEA assessments. It provides coverage of many of the methods and concepts in the curriculum but you should be aware that it is incomplete.

Teaching approach

The approach taken in this e-book is method-based and deals with the statistical concepts and techniques required at each level of the curriculum in a logical order. This approach is good for revision and reference but we do not recommend it for primary classroom teaching. In the classroom, a data-motivated approach is better.

Statistics is only important because it can answer important questions about real-life data and problems.

We therefore recommend that teachers introduce each topic with data sets and problems. Statistical methods should be introduced as required to deal with the data. The material in this e-book can then provide detailed information about these methods.

Most of the diagrams are also present in the companion e-book for NCEA students but that e-book is designed for self-study and contains much more descriptive text and exercises.
