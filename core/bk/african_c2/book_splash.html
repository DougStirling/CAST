This version of CAST is designed to accompany a second course in Statistics for students in Africa. It follows on from CAST for Africa: Exploring Data and assumes that readers are familiar with the earlier material.

The version contains material from the CAST web site, chosen to match a typical second course in Statistics in Africa and customised with some African data.

Information:
The topics in CAST for Africa may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.
