This version of CAST is designed to accompany a first course in Statistics for students in Africa.

The version contains material from the CAST web site, chosen to match a typical first course in Statistics in Africa and customised with some African data.

Information:
The topics in CAST for Africa may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.

A follow-on version of CAST explains 'randomness' in data, and describes statistical models for randomness and inference about these models.

This version of CAST was prepared in collaboration with Parin Kurji (University of Nairobi, Kenya) and Roger Stern (Statistical Services Centre, University of Reading, England). Customisation was partly funded by the Rockefeller Foundation.
