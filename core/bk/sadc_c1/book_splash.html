This is the first of three e-learning books and describes the structure of data sets and simple ways to summarise and describe a categorical variable.

The version contains selected material from the CAST web site.

Information:
If you are using CAST as part of an introductory statistics course, the topics in this version may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.

Follow-on versions of CAST explains numerical and graphical displays to explore data, and basic inference.

This version of CAST was prepared in collaboration with Roger Stern from the Statistical Services Centre at the University of Reading, England. Customisation was funded by the European Union.
