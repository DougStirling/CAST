Welcome to CAST for SADC, a version of CAST that is designed for workers involved with Official Statistics in Africa. This is the last of three e-learning books and follows on from e-books about exploring data with numerical and graphical summaries.

The version contains material from the CAST web site about the use of inference to answer questions about populations based on data from samples.

Information:
If you are using CAST as part of an introductory statistics course, the topics in this version may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.
