This CAST e-book has been designed to cover the statistical background needed for the projects in the first two years of the Bachelor of Engineering degree at Massey University.

The e-book uses data sets and scenarios from a wide range of applications of general interest. We have tried to use applications of relevance to engineering but others are from other areas of science or social sciences.

In some pages, you will notice a small triangle in the top right corner. This is a menu from which you can see different versions of the page with perhaps biological or business examples.
