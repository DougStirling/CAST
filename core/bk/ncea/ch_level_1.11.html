This chapter explains some statistical concepts related to the NCEA level 1 standard "1.11: Investigate bivariate numerical data using the statistical enquiry cycle".

This chapter describes some features that may be evident in scatterplots and explains how to interpret them.

The chapter is intended for self-study by students.

Assumed background

The pre-NCEA section Scatterplots of this e-book described how scatterplots could be used to display bivariate data; it should be revised before starting this section.

The material does not cover the complete curriculum. It has been provided as support material for parts of the curriculum.
