This chapter explains some statistical concepts related to the NCEA level 1 standard "1.12: Demonstrate understanding of chance and data". It contains material about the use of proportions and probability to describe randomness.

A short section about time series is also included here since the material is sometimes covered in this unit.

It is usually taught at Year 11 in New Zealand high schools and is assessed at NCEA Level 1.

The chapter is intended for self-study by students.

Assumed background

This chapter assumes a basic understanding of graphical displays of data but there are few specific topics that are required.

The material does not cover the complete curriculum. It has been provided as support material for parts of the curriculum.
