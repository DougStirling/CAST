This chapter explains some statistical concepts related to the NCEA level 2 standard "2.8: Design a questionnaire". It is usually taught in Year 12 in New Zealand high schools and is assessed at NCEA Level 2.

At present, the chapter does not include material about design of questionnaires but does give information about the mechanics of conducting a survey using such a questionnaire.

The chapter is intended for self-study by students.

Assumed background

The material in this chapter does not rely on any specific material earlier in CAST.

This chapter does not cover the complete curriculum. It has been provided as support material for parts of the curriculum.
