Welcome to the CAST e-book for students who are studying the New Zealand National Certificate of Educational Achievement (NCEA). The e-book provides a dynamic and interactive way to learn some important parts of the New Zealand curriculum and includes some exercises that will help the learning process.

Coverage of the curriculum

This e-book does not cover everything in the curriculum or that must be learned for the NCEA assessments. It provides coverage of many of the methods and concepts in the curriculum but you should be aware that it is incomplete.

Teaching approach

The approach taken in this e-book is method-based and deals with the statistical concepts and techniques required at each level of the curriculum in a logical order. This approach is good for revision and reference but we do not recommend it as the basis of classroom teaching. In the classroom, a data-motivated approach is better.

Statistics is only important because it can answer important questions about real-life data and problems.

We therefore recommend that teachers introduce each topic with data sets and problems. Statistical methods should be introduced as required to deal with the data. The material in this e-book can then provide detailed information about these methods.

This e-book is intended for self-study and is fairly self-contained. A companion e-book for NCEA teachers contains only the interactive diagrams and is intended for classroom use with a data projector.
