Welcome to CAST for Climatology, a version of CAST that is designed to accompany a first course in Statistics for researchers and workers in climatology.

The version contains material from the CAST web site, customised with some climate data.

Information:
If you are using CAST as part of an introductory statistics course, the topics in this version may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.

Follow-on versions of CAST explains 'randomness' in data, and describes statistical models for randomness and inference about these models.

This version of CAST was prepared in collaboration with Roger Stern from the Statistical Services Centre at the University of Reading, England. Customisation was partly funded by the UK Met Office and Department for International Development.
