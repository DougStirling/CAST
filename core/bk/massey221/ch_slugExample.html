The case study in this chapter develops a statistical model for a data set that describes physical characteristics of slugs. Simple models are initially fitted to the data set, and diagnostic plots are used to highlight problems. A nonlinear model is eventually discovered that fits the data well and it is used to gain information about the body shapes of the slugs.

Several of the methods that were learnt in the previous chapter are used in the analysis.

The pages in this chapter were initially written as a separate CAST module. They contain some buttons that display additional information in an extra window, but most of this extra information is already contained in the previous chapter.
