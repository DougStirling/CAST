This CAST e-book has been designed to accompany the paper 161.130 Introductory Biostatistics at Massey University. The material is structured into chapters that complement those of the textbook for 161.130 (by Clark and Randal).

The e-book uses data sets and scenarios from the biological, health and agricultural sciences.

In some pages, you will notice a small triangle in the top right corner. This is a menu from which you can see different versions of the page with perhaps business or social science examples.
