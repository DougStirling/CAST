This CAST e-book has been designed to accompany the paper 161.100 Principles of Statistics at Massey University. The material is structured into chapters that match those of the Study Guide for 161.100.

Warning:
Some chapters of this version of CAST go into more detail than the corresponding chapters of the Study Guide. The Study Guide defines what you will need for the course.

The version uses data sets and scenarios from a wide range of applications of general interest.

In some pages, you will notice a small triangle in the top right corner. This is a menu from which you can see different versions of the page with perhaps biological or business examples.
