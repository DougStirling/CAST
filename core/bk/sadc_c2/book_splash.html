Welcome to CAST for SADC, a version of CAST that is designed for workers involved with Official Statistics in Africa. This is the second of three e-learning books and follows on from a short e-book about the structure of data sets and simple ways to summarise and describe a categorical variable.

The version contains material from the CAST web site about numerical and graphical methods to explore data.

Information:
If you are using CAST as part of an introductory statistics course, the topics in this version may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.

A follow-on version of CAST will describe statistical methods for inference.
