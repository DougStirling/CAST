Welcome to CAST for SADC, a version of CAST that is designed for workers involved with Official Statistics in Africa.

This is the fourth e-book in the CAST for SADC series and contains advanced material from other versions of CAST that does not appear in the first three SADC e-books.

This e-book is a collection of sections that has not yet been structured into a coherent course of study but may be of interest to some readers.

As with the other e-books in the series, this e-book contains material from the CAST web site.

Information:
If you are using CAST as part of an introductory statistics course, the topics in this version may not correspond exactly to the material in your actual Statistics course. Your lecturer or tutor will explain which parts of CAST to study.
