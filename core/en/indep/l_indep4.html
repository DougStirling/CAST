Simulation from independent model

This page uses a simulation to show that the 'obvious' test statistic, , has a distribution that depends on the sample size, but that the distribution of the χ2 statistic depends only on the size of the contingency table (assuming independence).

Poor statistic

With the 'poor' test statistic, take a few samples and observe the variability in the differences and their sum of squares. Increase the sample size and repeat. The differences and their sum of squares are higher.
