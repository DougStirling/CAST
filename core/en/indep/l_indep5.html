Shape of the chi-squared distribution

Firstly state that χ2 has (approx) a chi-squared distribution with (r - 1)(c - 1) degrees of freedom.

Use the pop-up menus to change r and c and investigate how the χ2 distribution depends on the degrees of freedom. Observe that the distribution:

  • ... is skew, but becomes closer to symmetric when the degrees of freedom are large.
  • ... has mean equal its degrees of freedom.
