Estimating the mean of a skew distribution

Use this diagram to make the point that most estimators are unbiased, but not all are. The simulation compares the use of the sample mean and median as estimators of the mean of a skew distribution (exponential).

The diagram shows the (approx) theoretical distns of the sample mean and median. Increase the sample size to see how they change.

  • Both estimators have similar standard errors
  • The sample median is biased — the mean of its error distribution is negative.
