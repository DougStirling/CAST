Body fat of AIS athletes

Explain that a separate least squares line can be fitted to each group. Select Separate lines from the pop-up menu.

The separate lines allow us to estimate Body fat more accurately using both BMI and Sex.

Data were collected from a sample of 202 elite athletes who were in training at the Australian Institute of Sport. The variables used on this page are:

  • Body fat (%), obtained by an underwater weighing technique
  • Body mass index, BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)2
  • Sex
