Rice yields

Firstly click Colour groups to use different colours (and symbols) to distinguish between countries in five different regions of the world. The regions are still difficult to compare.

Click Animate grouping to separate the regions vertically. Click on crosses to identify countries and explain that:

  • Rice yields are extremely low in Africa, with the exception of 2 outliers (Morocco and Egypt) which are both in North Africa.
  • Rice yields are also low in 'West and North Asia'.
  • There is a wide spread of yields in 'East Asia and Australia'.

The data set contains rice yields (tonnes/hectare) in all major rice-producing countries in 1996/97.

In the regional grouping that was used,

  • Australia has been added to East Asia
  • Central and South America have been combined
  • North Asia has been combined with West Asia
