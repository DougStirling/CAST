Sepal and petal dimensions of irises

Use this diagram to discuss the use of scatterplot matrices.

Features to mention are:

  • Two separate clusters in most plots.
  • Strong relationship between petal length and width, but not sepal length and width.
  • Petal length is strongly associated with sepal length.

Click Use brush and drag over crosses to confirm that the clusters are largely the same in all scatterplots.

The data are measurements of the sepals (length and width) and petals (length and width) of 150 irises that were collected from the Gaspe Peninsula in Canada.
