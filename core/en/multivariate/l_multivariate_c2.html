Dow Jones and NASDAQ (15 January to 17 April 2002)

The diagram initially shows a time series plot of the Dow Jones share price. Click Join crosses to display the trend more clearly.

The diagram is 3-dimensional and also shows the NASDAQ share price index each day. Rotate by dragging the centre of the scatterplot with the mouse or using the buttons.

The data are the Dow Jones and NASDAQ share indices on all trading days between 15 January and 17 April 2002. In the scatterplot, 15 January is labeled day 1.
