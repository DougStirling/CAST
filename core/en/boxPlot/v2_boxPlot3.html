Box plots and histograms

There is a close relationship between histogram area and box plots. The four regions of a box plot correspond to approximately equal areas in a histogram of the data.

The relationship between histogram and box plot shape should help you to understand what a box plot is telling you about the distribution of values.

What does a box plot tell you about the distribution?

A box plot conveys information about the centre of the distribution, the spread of values and the amount of skewness.

From any box plot, you should now have a reasonable impression of the distribution of values, and should be able to sketch the corresponding histogram.
