Stacked dot plots

Graphical a fair amount of information about the shape of a distribution. In particular, it clearly shows symmetry and skewness.

The following exercise shows stacked dot plots for four data sets on the right. Export the data, produce appropriate graphical displays of the four data sets then match the stacked dot plots with the data sets by dragging the stacked dot plots.

Histograms

This exercise is similar but shows histograms of the data in the exercise. Also, the data are exported in "stacked" format with a single column of values and a column of factor levels distinguishing between the groups.

Again drag the diagrams to match ones corresponding to the same data.
