Students usually find the standard deviation a difficult concept because of its formula. Luckily, understanding its definition is much less important than having a feel for what its numerical value means.

Standard deviation and histogram

From any histogram, you should be able to make a rough guess at the value of the standard deviation.

Similarly, given the mean and standard deviation for a data set, you should be able to draw a rough sketch of a symmetric histogram with that mean and standard deviation.
