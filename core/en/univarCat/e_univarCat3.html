Ordering categories by 'importance'

A Pareto diagram is a bar chart in which the categories are ordered by decreasing frequency. Even though the categories are usually not be naturally ordered (i.e. it is not an ordinal categorical variable), cumulative proportions are usually added to the bar chart.

In the following exercise, you should drag the bars of the bar chart to reorder them into a Pareto diagram, then use the Pareto diagram to answer a question about the most important or least important categories.
