Wine production in New Zealand

Use this example to explain the need to think carefully about the variables to tabulate (and display graphically).

The column showing total wine production is useful but hides an important trend. Click Show yield to add columns with the area used for wine production and the ratio of production to area. Use the '-' button to decrease the number of decimals to one — two significant digits are enough to assess the trend.

  • Although wine production varied considerably from year to year (presumably because of the weather), the yield per hectare has decreased from 1986 to 2001.
