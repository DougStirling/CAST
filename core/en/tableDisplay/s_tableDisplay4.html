Ratios

It is sometimes better to divide values by some measure of 'size' before analysis or display.

  • Values from countries such as GDP or gross production of items can usually be compared better if divided by population size to give a 'per capita' value.
  • If financial quantities are tracked over years in which there is inflation, they are usually divided by a price index such as the consumer price index (CPI) in order to see how the 'value' of the quantities changes
