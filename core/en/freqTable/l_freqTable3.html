Road crashes causing injury or death

Use the diagram to discuss a few different ways to modify a frequency table:

  • Click the checkboxes Sort by frequency. Alphabetical ordering rarely helps you to extract information from a table. Pareto ordering (by frequency) is usually better.
  • Click Combine categories to group the categories for Intersections or Non-intersections.
  • Click Hide categories for either Intersections or Non-intersections. This shows conditional proportions.

The data set contains the number of road crashes causing injury or death in New Zealand in 2005, categorised by the type of 'road feature' at the crash site.

The 'road features' were grouped into Intersections and Non-intersections in the report and are shown in different colours in the table.
