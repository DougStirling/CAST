Simple stem and leaf plot

Guinea Pig Survival data (split stems)

The main value of stem and leaf plots is that they can be easily drawn by hand.

In both examples,

  • Click on each value in the list to add its leaf to the appropriate stem.
  • Click Sort leaves to sort the leaves into increasing order. (The leaves are usually sorted.)

In the second example, some digits of the values are truncated and the stems are split.

The second data set gives survival times (in days) of 72 guinea pigs that were injected with tubercle bacilli.
