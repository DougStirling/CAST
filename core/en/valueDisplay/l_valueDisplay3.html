Residence in nursing homes in the USA among those 65+

Click crosses to identify the states and exact values.

Choose Digits from the pop-up menu to replace the crosses with digits. These allow you to read off the 'tenths' digits of the values — e.g. to distinguish between Michigan (18.0) and Wyoming (18.9).

(This is an introduction to stem and leaf plots.)

The values describe the nursing home residents in different states of the USA. The units are the number per 500 people aged 65+ in each state.
