Normal population (mean 12 and sd 2)

Use the simulation to show that sample means have a distribution and that:

  • When n = 1, mean has same distribution as the population.
  • For all n, mean's distribution is centred on the population mean, 12.
  • Variability of the mean decreases as the sample size increases.

Click Accumulate then take several samples to build up the sampling distn of the mean when the sample size is 16.

Repeat with different sample sizes to demonstrate how the sample size affects the spread of the mean's distribution.
