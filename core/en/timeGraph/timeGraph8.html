Showing change on a map

The static maps that were described in an earlier chapter can also be turned into dynamic diagrams if they are displayed on a computer.

Calorie intake in Africa

The circles on the African map below show the populations of the countries (circle areas) and the average calorie intake (Kcal/capita/day) in each country.

The initial display shows clearly the regions in which food calorie intake was highest (blue) and lowest (yellow) in 1990. Drag the slider to see how this changed between 1990 and 2005. Observe in particular:

  • The decreasing food intake in Libya and many countries in Southern Africa (except Angola and South Africa).
  • The rapidly increasing food intake in Ghana.
