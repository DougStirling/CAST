Find a power transformation

The family of power transformations includes the log transformation. They change the shape of a distribution and are often used to make a skew distribution more symmetric.

In the exercise below, export the data create variables that contain power transformations of the original values and plot them (ideally with dot plots). Identify which transformation gives a distribution that matches the 'symmetric' distribution shown under the original data set.

Note that the biggest value becomes the smallest (and vice versa) when a negative power is used. (Click crosses on the dot plots to see how the untransformed and transformed values are related.
