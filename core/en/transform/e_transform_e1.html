Mean and standard deviation

The mean and standard deviation of a set of linearly transformed values can be calculated from the mean and standard deviation before transformation.

There are two possible ways to answer the question below -- try both. You can either:

  • export the data, create a new variable with the transformed values then find its mean and standard deviation, or
  • export the data, find its mean and standard deviation, then transform the mean and standard deviation into the required units.
