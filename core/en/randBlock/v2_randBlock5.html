Blocks and treatments

There are major differences between the concepts of blocks and treatments:

  • Blocks are a grouping of the experimental units whereas treatments are allocated by the researcher.
  • We are mainly interested in comparing the treatments. Variability between blocks is nuisance variation that we often try to avoid.

However from a model-building perspective, blocks and treatments are modelled in the same way.
