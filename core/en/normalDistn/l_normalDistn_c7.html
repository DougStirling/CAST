This diagram provides three templates that can be used to evaluate the following. (Use the pop-up menu to change the template.)

  • P(X < a)
  • P(X > b)
  • P(c < X < d)

In each case, the normal parameters can be edited (type into the boxes on the left). The constants (a, b, or c&d) can also be edited. The diagram changes to find the relevant z-score(s) and area under the standard normal pdf.
