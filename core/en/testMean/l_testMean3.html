Distribution of t test statistic

Use this diagram to demonstrate that the t- test statistic has greater spread than the standard normal distribution, especially when the sample size is small.

  • Click Accumulate then take about 50 random samples of n = 20. The distribution of t statistic conforms reasonably with a standard normal distribution.
  • Reduce the sample size to n = 5 and take 100 more samples. Values of t less than -3 or more than +3 occur more often than expected from a standard normal distribution.
  • Reduce the sample size to n = 3 and repeat. It should be clear that t has greater spread than a standard normal distribution.
  • Click on the crosses for the most extreme t-values. They usually arise when the 3 sample values are close together and s is small.
