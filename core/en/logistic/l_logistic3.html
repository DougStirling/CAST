Logistic curve

Use this diagram to describe the shape of the family of logistic curves and its parameters.

The constants b0 and b1 are similar to the parameters of a straight line. Use the sliders to adjust their values.

  • The intercept shifts the logistic curve horizontally.
  • The slope affects the steepness of the curve.
  • When the slope is positive, the predicted proportion increases with x.
  • Changing the slope does not affect the predicted proportion at x = 0.
