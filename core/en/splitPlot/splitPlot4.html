Factor means

Consider a split plot experiment with factor A allocated at block level and factor B varied within blocks. If factor B's levels are used in the same proportion within all blocks, tables of factor means (or graphical display of these means) can be used to summarise the effects of the factors.

No interaction between A and B
The main effects of the two factors are summarised by the mean responses at each level of the two factors.
Interaction between A and B
If there is interaction between the two factors, a two-way table of means for all combinations of factor levels is needed to describe their effects.
