CAST?

CAST stands for Computer-Assisted Statistics Textbooks and is a collection of electronic textbooks (e-books). Three e-books teach introductory statistical methods with data and scenarios from different application areas. Other e-books teach more advanced topics.

Features and approach

  • CAST is highly interactive. Interaction with diagrams on most pages retains attention and enhances learning.
     
  • Dynamic graphics explain difficult concepts. Animations, simulations and other dynamic displays are extensively used.
     
  • The approach is data-focused. Learners are exposed to many data sets.
     
  • Some advanced topics are included. With a computer-based approach, readers can learn topics that were once considered 'advanced'.
