Probabilities in a sub-population

Complete population
The joint probabilities pxy and the marginal probabilities px and py all describe proportions in the complete population of (x, y) pairs.
Sub-population
In contrast, it is sometimes meaningful to restrict attention to a subset of the (x, y) pairs. For example, we may be interested only in pairs for which the first variable, X , has some particular value. Probabilities that relate to a sub-population are called conditional probabilities.
