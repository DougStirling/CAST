Relationship between joint, marginal and conditional probabilities

A model can be equivalently described by any of the following.

  • The joint probabilities, pxy
  • The marginal probabilities for X, px, and the conditional probabilities for Y, py | x
  • The marginal probabilities for Y, py, and the conditional probabilities for X, px | y

From any of these sets of probabilities, it is possible to find the other two sets.
