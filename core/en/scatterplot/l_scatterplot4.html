Lifespan and metabolic rate of mammals

A standard scatterplot can be enhanced with marginal displays of the individual variables against the axes.

Use the pop-up menu to show some alternative ways to display the marginal distributions.

  • The marginal distributions are rarely displayed in these ways.
  • The marginal distributions do not add extra information, they only make some information that is already in the scatterplot more prominent.

The data set contains the lifespans of various mammals (in years) and their metabolic rates (recorded in terms of oxygen consumption per gram of body weight).

  • Both the marginal distributions of lifespan and that of metabolic rate are very skew.
