Tourist arrivals in Fiji

Tourist arrivals in most destinations are highly seasonal. In these data, there is a repeating pattern of higher numbers from July to September of each year — a seasonal pattern.

The data set gives the total number of tourists arriving in Fiji each month between January 2004 and December 2007.

In most tourist destinations, there is a single peak in the summer of each year but in Fiji, the peak is in the winter months of July to September which have less rainfall and are cooler.
